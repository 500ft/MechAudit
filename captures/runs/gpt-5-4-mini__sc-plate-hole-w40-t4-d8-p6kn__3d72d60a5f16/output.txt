Use

\[
\sigma_{\max}=K_t\frac{P}{Wt}
\]

For a central circular hole in a finite-width plate,

\[
K_t \approx 3-3.14\left(\frac dW\right)+3.667\left(\frac dW\right)^2-1.527\left(\frac dW\right)^3
\]

With \(d/W=8/40=0.2\),

\[
K_t \approx 2.51
\]

Nominal stress:

\[
\frac{P}{Wt}=\frac{6000}{40\times 4}=37.5\ \text{MPa}
\]

So,

\[
\sigma_{\max}\approx 2.51(37.5)\approx 94\ \text{MPa}
\]

Final answer: \(\boxed{\sigma_{\max}\approx 94\ \text{MPa}}\)
