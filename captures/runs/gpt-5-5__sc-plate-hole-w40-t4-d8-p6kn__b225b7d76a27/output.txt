For a finite-width plate with a central circular hole:

\[
\sigma_{\max}=K_t \sigma_{\text{nom}}
\]

where

\[
\sigma_{\text{nom}}=\frac{P}{(W-d)t}
\]

Given:

\[
\sigma_{\text{nom}}=\frac{6000}{(40-8)(4)}
=\frac{6000}{128}
=46.875\text{ MPa}
\]

For \(d/W=8/40=0.2\), use \(K_t \approx 2.5\).

\[
\sigma_{\max}\approx 2.5(46.875)=117.2\text{ MPa}
\]

\[
\boxed{\sigma_{\max}\approx 117\text{ MPa}}
\]
