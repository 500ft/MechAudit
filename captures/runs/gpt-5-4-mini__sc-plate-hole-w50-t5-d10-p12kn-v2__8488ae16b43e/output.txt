Use the stress-concentration formula for a plate with a central circular hole:

\[
\sigma_{\max}=K_t \frac{P}{Wt}
\]

For \(d/W=10/50=0.2\),

\[
K_t \approx 3-3.13\left(\frac dW\right)+3.66\left(\frac dW\right)^2-1.53\left(\frac dW\right)^3 \approx 2.51
\]

Nominal stress:

\[
\frac{P}{Wt}=\frac{12\,000}{50 \times 5}=48\ \text{MPa}
\]

So,

\[
\sigma_{\max}\approx 2.51 \times 48 \approx 120\ \text{MPa}
\]

Final answer: \(\boxed{\sigma_{\max}\approx 120\ \text{MPa}}\)
