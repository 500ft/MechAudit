Formula:

\[
\sigma_{\max}=K_t \sigma_{\text{nom}}, \qquad 
\sigma_{\text{nom}}=\frac{P}{(W-d)t}
\]

For a central circular hole in a finite-width plate:

\[
K_t=3-3.14\frac dW+3.667\left(\frac dW\right)^2-1.527\left(\frac dW\right)^3
\]

\[
\frac dW=\frac{16}{80}=0.2,\qquad K_t \approx 2.51
\]

\[
\sigma_{\text{nom}}=\frac{20{,}000}{(80-16)(5)}
=\frac{20{,}000}{320}=62.5\text{ MPa}
\]

\[
\sigma_{\max}\approx 2.51(62.5)\approx 157\text{ MPa}
\]

Final answer: **\(\boxed{\sigma_{\max}\approx 157\text{ MPa}}\)**.
