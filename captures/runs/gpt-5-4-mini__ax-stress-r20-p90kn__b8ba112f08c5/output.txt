Use the axial stress formula:

\[
\sigma = \frac{P}{A}, \quad A = \pi r^2
\]

Given:
- \(P = 90\,\text{kN} = 90{,}000\,\text{N}\)
- \(r = 20\,\text{mm}\)

Area:
\[
A = \pi (20)^2 = 400\pi \approx 1256.6\ \text{mm}^2
\]

Stress:
\[
\sigma = \frac{90{,}000}{1256.6} \approx 71.6\ \text{N/mm}^2
\]

Final answer:
\[
\boxed{\sigma \approx 71.6\ \text{MPa}}
\]
