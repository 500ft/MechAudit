Formula:

\[
\sigma = \frac{P}{A} = \frac{P}{\pi r^2}
\]

With \(P = 90\,\text{kN} = 90{,}000\,\text{N}\) and \(r = 20\,\text{mm}\):

\[
A = \pi(20)^2 = 400\pi \,\text{mm}^2
\]

\[
\sigma = \frac{90{,}000}{400\pi} = \frac{225}{\pi}\,\text{N/mm}^2 \approx 72\,\text{MPa}
\]

Final answer:

\[
\boxed{\sigma \approx 72\,\text{MPa}}
\]
