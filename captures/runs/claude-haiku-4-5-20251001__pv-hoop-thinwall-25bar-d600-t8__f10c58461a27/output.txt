**Formula:**

For a thin-walled cylindrical pressure vessel, the hoop stress is:

$$\sigma_h = \frac{P \cdot d}{2t}$$

where P is internal pressure, d is inner diameter, and t is wall thickness.

**Solution:**

Convert pressure: 25 bar = 2.5 MPa

$$\sigma_h = \frac{2.5 \text{ MPa} \times 600 \text{ mm}}{2 \times 8 \text{ mm}} = \frac{1500}{16} = 93.75 \text{ MPa}$$

**Answer: 93.75 MPa**
