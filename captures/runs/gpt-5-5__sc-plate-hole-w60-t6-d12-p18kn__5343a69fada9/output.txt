Formula:

\[
\sigma_{\max}=K_t \frac{P}{Wt}
\]

For a finite-width plate with a central circular hole:

\[
K_t=3-3.14\frac dW+3.667\left(\frac dW\right)^2-1.527\left(\frac dW\right)^3
\]

\[
\frac dW=\frac{12}{60}=0.2,\qquad \frac{P}{Wt}=\frac{18000}{60\cdot6}=50\text{ MPa}
\]

\[
K_t \approx 2.51
\]

\[
\sigma_{\max}\approx 2.51(50)=125\text{ MPa}
\]

Final answer: **\(\sigma_{\max}\approx 125\text{ MPa}\)**.
