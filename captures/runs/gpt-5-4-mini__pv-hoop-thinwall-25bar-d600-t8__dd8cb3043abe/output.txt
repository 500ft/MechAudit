Use the thin-cylinder hoop stress formula:

\[
\sigma_h = \frac{p d}{2t}
\]

Given:
- \(p = 25\ \text{bar} = 2.5\ \text{N/mm}^2\)
- \(d = 600\ \text{mm}\)
- \(t = 8\ \text{mm}\)

\[
\sigma_h = \frac{2.5 \times 600}{2 \times 8} = \frac{1500}{16} = 93.75\ \text{N/mm}^2
\]

Final answer: \(\boxed{93.75\ \text{MPa}}\)
